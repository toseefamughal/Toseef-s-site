ABOUT ME

I believe in living life to the fullest and not letting anyone tell you that you can not accomplish anything. Growing up as an amputee I did not have many people to look up to. I want to be that person that can motivate and inspire others to get out of their comfort zone and challenge themselves, especially amputees and people with disabilities. I also believe that living a healthy lifestyle is maintaining a strong and fit body. My aim is to help identify your goals and build you a training program that best fits your needs. Contact me today and let’s get in the best shape of our lives together!

MY PROGRAMS

MORE ENERGY

The purpose of personal training is to feel more energetic. Exercise stimulates your body, forcing it to adapt and make changes, helping you not only look fitter on the outside but have more energy naturally on the inside.

CONFIDENCE BOOST

The side effects of ageing can leave you feeling unhappy with the way you look. My strength training plans will see you lose body fat, build strong muscles to see you feeling confident and wearing your favorite clothes.

QUALITY OF LIFE

Expert personal training will improve your quality of life by giving you the fitness and the energy to play with children, enjoy outdoor sports, and feel fresh after a hard day’s work. The fitter you are, the more of life’s joys you can experience.
